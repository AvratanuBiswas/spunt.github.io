Welcome. I use experimental psychology and functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) to investigate social cognition and emotional experience in humans like you and me, and lately have become especially interested in learning more about how and why people differ. I have a Ph.D. in Social Psychology from UCLA and there worked in the labs of Matt Lieberman and Naomi Eisenberger. I am currently finishing up a postdoctoral scientist fellowship in Ralph Adolphs’ Lab at Caltech. To learn more, two reasonable places to start are my Resume and CV.
