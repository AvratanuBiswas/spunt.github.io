Welcome. I use experimental psychology and functional magnetic resonance imaging to learn about social expertise and emotional experience in humans like you and me, and lately have become especially interested in learning more about how and why people differ. I have a Ph.D. in Social Psychology from UCLA and there worked in the labs of Matt Lieberman and Naomi Eisenberger. I am now a postdoctoral fellow in Ralph Adolphs’ Lab at Caltech. Check out my CV and papers to find out more about my research.
